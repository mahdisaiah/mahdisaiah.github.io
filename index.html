ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Saiah Mahdi, a 22-year-old 3D Generalist and Game Designer with a passion for crafting immersive digital worlds. I thrive on bringing ideas to life through detailed environments, captivating characters, and innovative gameplay mechanics. My toolkit includes industry-standard software like Blender, Maya, Cinema4D, and Substance Painter, paired with game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine. I’m also skilled in video editing with Premiere Pro, adding a cinematic touch to my projects.

I’m deeply fascinated by the intersection of gaming and cutting-edge technology. Whether it’s PC, VR, or mobile game development, I’m always exploring new ways to push boundaries and create unforgettable experiences. I’m particularly excited about the integration of artificial intelligence into gaming—imagine smarter NPCs, dynamic storytelling, and adaptive gameplay that evolves with the player. My academic background in game development, 3D modeling, and cinema fuels my creativity, while my curiosity drives me to experiment with emerging tech and collaborate on groundbreaking projects.

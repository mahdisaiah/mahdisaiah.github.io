Selected work 01 Game project

Empreintes

Unity/ Blender/ C#

An open world experience about human impact on the environment. You move through a landscape shaped by human traces, roads, machines, extraction and ruins, with nature slowly fighting back.

RoleDesign & development
TypeUniversity & indie
Year2024 – 2025
01.1 In motion

Four sides

01.2 The idea

The world
as the message

Empreintes starts from a simple question: what does a place look like once people have moved through it? You explore an open world marked by our passage, roads cut into the landscape, abandoned machines, extraction sites and ruins, while nature patiently takes the ground back.

I worked on the world design, the gameplay systems and the vehicles in Unity, with assets modeled in Blender and logic written in C#. The point is not to lecture, it is to let the environment carry the message.
